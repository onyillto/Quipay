**Quipay** is an autonomous, on-chain payroll streaming protocol built on Stellar Soroban. It enables employers to fund a treasury vault and stream salaries directly to workers in real time — no intermediaries, no delays, and no banks required.

<!-- truncate -->

## Why Quipay?

Traditional payroll systems are slow, expensive, and fragmented. Quipay replaces the monthly payroll cycle with continuous, second-by-second salary accrual settled on the Stellar network:

- **PayrollStream contract** — streams funds from employer treasury to worker wallets in real time
- **PayrollVault contract** — custodian for employer funds with on-chain solvency enforcement
- **Workforce Registry** — decentralized worker profile registry with preferred token selection
- **Automation Gateway** — permissioned agent framework for AI-driven payroll automation (Phase 2)

## Built on Stellar

Quipay leverages Soroban's low-latency, low-cost execution environment to make continuous payroll economically viable. All fund movements are transparent, auditable, and self-custodied.
